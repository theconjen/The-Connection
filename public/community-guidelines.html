Community Guidelines

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

The Connection is built to foster respectful, faith-centered conversations. To keep this a safe and welcoming place, we ask everyone to follow these simple rules:

Faith Identity

This app is unapologetically Christian. We welcome users of all backgrounds to participate respectfully and even debate, but:

  • Non-Christian communities will not be supported.
  • Proselytizing or organizing for other religions or ideologies is not allowed.
  • Respectful questions, dialogue, and debate from non-Christians are welcome, provided the purpose of the app is honored.

What’s Not Allowed

  • Threats of violence or bodily harm.
  • Pornography or sexually explicit material (lifetime ban).
  • Harassment, targeted hate, or bullying.
  • Posting disinformation (objectively false information shared to mislead or harm).
  • Illegal activity of any kind.

What Is Allowed

  • Free speech: honest opinions, respectful disagreements, and robust debate.
  • Constructive discussions on faith, culture, and life.
  • Reporting content if you feel unsafe, threatened, or misled.

Enforcement

We may delete content, suspend accounts, or issue permanent bans when these guidelines are broken. Users who feel unsafe can report posts or block other users at any time.

Contact

If you see something that violates these guidelines, report it in-app or email: abuse@theconnection.app
